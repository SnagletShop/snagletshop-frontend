Sales-optimized setup
  • Goal clarity: one primary goal (free shipping or tier discount). Too many goals lowers conversion.
  • First step close: first unlock usually performs best at ~10–15% above typical order subtotal.
  • Low-risk add-ons: light, universal items; keep top-up items within ~30% price delta by default.
  • Single checkout upsell: avoid grids at checkout—show one best add-on only.
  • A/B test: threshold, copy (“unlock” vs “save”), and placement (cart vs checkout).
This panel is guidance only; it does not affect storefront behavior.
